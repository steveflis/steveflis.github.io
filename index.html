About Flis Forest & Land

Steve Flis, a Central Pennsylvania native, founded Flis Forest & Land, LLC out of a lifelong passion for forest restoration and private land improvement. With a Bachelor’s in Forest Ecosystem Management from Penn State, ISA arborist certification, commercial pesticide license, and years of hands-on experience, Steve is committed to improving the value, health, and ecological function of Pennsylvania’s 12 million acres of privately owned forests.
